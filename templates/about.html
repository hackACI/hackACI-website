About hAckCI

About Us

hAckCI was founded by a group of high school students attending Agincourt CI.
Our mission is to create a community where talented people can connect, share, and develop their creative skills to a new level. You will present your amazing project to the judges where you are able to compete for prizes. Form a team and build a project from start to finish! hAckCI is for everyone, and you do not need to come from a technical background. Join 50+ high school hackers on a journey to make your projects come to life. We will provide you with workshops, ideas and support you on your journey of creativity.

What does hAckCI do?

We create and manage events that highlight creativity and encourage new ideas to come to light in many ways. Our last event was ACI's first ever hackathon, and we plan to have new events in the future.

Our Values

Here at hAckCI, we take pride in upholding our values, which include:

  • Innovation

    We prioritize creativity and encourage out-of-the-box thinking to solve complex problems and create efficient solutions.

  • Integrity

    We operate with honesty and transparency in all our actions.

  • Inclusivity

    We embrace diversity and strive to create a safe and inclusive environment where everyone is valued with equal opportunities to contribute and grow.

Frequently Asked Questions

How to apply?
Login and create an account! There will be a designated area to signup for the event there.
Who can participate?
Agincourt Collegiate students from any grade are allowed to participate in this hackathon. Unfortunately, it is not open to other schools this year.
Do I need to know how to code?
No, anyone from any skill level is welcome to participate. Your project will be judged on creativity, presentation, and technicality combined.
