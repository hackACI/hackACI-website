Our Sponsors

We are deeply greatful to the sponsors listed below for their generous contributions of time, goods and funds - all of which allow us to make these experiences possible for you.

Wolfram

Wolfram Alpha is a computer, web and cloud software company. Their goal is to provide the framework to let computation achieve its full potential in the decades to come: to make it possible to compute whatever can be computed, whenever and wherever it is needed, and to make accessible the full frontiers of the computational universe.

Scarborough Center for Healthy Communities

The mission of Scarborough Center for Healthy Communities is to provide accessible, equitable and transformational health and social service choices for the well-being of the diverse community.

Elegoo

This company sells leading technology for the future innovators. They encourage the use of 3D printers and programming, which is closely linked to our hackathon, a programming and innovative thinking competition.

Echo3D

Echo3D is a software company that aims to help developers and organizations manage and store 3D content securely and be able to share and stream in real-time. This company provides a platform programmers can use to create their projects and work collaboratively more easily. This hackathon puts a large emphasis on working collaboratively, this platform encourages equity and inclusion, allowing more people to easily access projects.

Interview Cake

Interview Cake is a company that provides courses related to coding that help prepare people for interviews. This would encourage and help students wishing to pursue careers in software and computer science.

Gen.xyz

Gen.xyz is a company that sells .xyz domains, which are unique and eliminate the label/language barrier. They will be able to provide students with a free domain, which can be used to apply their coding skills to create a website.

TMU

Toronto Metropolitan University (TMU), located in Downtown Toronto, is a school that encourages diversity, entrepreneurship and innovation.
